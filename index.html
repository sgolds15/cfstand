Canadian Health Obituaries Index File

This database lists obituaries or death notices of Canadian medical personal that appeared in various medical journals listed below between 1844 and 2000. In cases where the actual journal is available online, the title of the journal has a hyperlink. Otherwise, you will need to contact a library to get the actual obituary or notice.

The seachable database contains listings for obituaries and notices of death which appeared in:

  • British American Journal (BAJ) v. 1–3, 1860–1862
  • British American Journal of Medical & Physical Science (BAJM) v. 1–5, 1845–1850
  • British American Medical and Physical Journal (BAMP) v. 6–7, 1850–1852
  • Canadian Journal of Medical Science (CJMS) v. 1–7, 1876–1882
  • Canada Lancet (CL) v. 1–2, 1863–1864
  • Canadian Medical Association Journal (CMAJ) v. 1–163, 1911–2000
  • Canada Medical Journal and Monthly Record (CMJ) v. 1, 1852–1853
  • Canada Medical Journal and Monthly Record (CMJM) v. 1–8, 1864–1872
  • Canada Medical Record (CMR) v. 1–32, 1872–1904
  • Canada Medical and Surgical Journal (CMSJ) v. 1–16, 1872–1888
  • Canadian Practitioner (CP) v. 8–23, 1833–1898
  • Canadian Practitioner and Review (CPR) v. 24–35, 1899–1910
  • Dominion Medical Journal (DMJ) v. 1–2, 1868–1870
  • Dosimétrie au Canada (DC) v. 1–3, 1899–1901
  • Kingston Medical Quarterly (KMQ) v. 1–7, 1896–1903

  • Maritime Medical News (MMN) v. 1–22, 1889–1910

  • Union Médicale du Canada (UMC) v. 1–124, 1872–1995
